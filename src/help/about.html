Welcome to PowerJ

PowerJ is a data mining software and dashboard application to help pathologists, management and end-users in the following areas:

  • Insuring smooth workflow and processing of cases and reports in a timely fashion.

  • Help pathology management to make informed decisions on specimen allocation, bottlenecks and case tracking.

  • Measure workload for the entire department, by subspecialty and individual pathologists.

  • A living schedule management with monthly and annual distribution reports by subspecialty and pathologist.
