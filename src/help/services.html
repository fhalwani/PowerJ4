PowerJ Setup - Services

These are the subunits of the clinical service schedule. You may require 1 to 5 pathologists to cover the GI service (GI-1, GI-2, GI-3, GI-4, GI-5). If the 5th pathologist will also cover liver, then the service can be called GI-Liv. The services names will display in the schedule in alphabetical order. Each service has the following entries:

  • Name: the name that will display in the Schedule report.

  • Description: the duties covered by the service.

  • Days: select the days of the week that need to be covered. If you select call, it implies the entire week, including Saturday & Sunday.

  • Active: If de-selected, the service will not appear in the schedule.

  • Sub: If selected, the service is part of a subspecialty. If de-selected, the service is part of a specialty without a subspecialty (eg, cytology service)

  • Facility: the facility that the service belongs to (for multi-facility institutes).

  • Subspecialty: the subspecialty that the service belongs to.

  • Specialty: the specialty that the service belongs to.


To add a new service, scroll to the bottom of the list on the left side of the screen. Then select the blank row at the bottom before proceeding to enter the appropriate data on the right side of the screen.
