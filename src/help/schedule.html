Schedule

This screen displays the clinical service schedule of the pathologists on a weekly basis. It can be viewed by service or by pathologist. It can display up to 12 weeks in the future and up to 40 weeks in the past. You can also get a summary of the schedule distribution over a year, or parts of a year in the Workdays report.


Data Entry:

The person with the proper access to the schedule setup can add new weeks to the schedule, or modify the current schedule if it requires adjustments.


Filter toolbar:

The second toolbar on top of the screen below the quick access toolbar and above the charts, allows you to filter the schedule by facility.


Spin:

This button switches the view between services and pathologists.
