Turnaround Time

This screen displays the average time to complete a task in business hours, starting from accession time. The time is cumulative, meaning the embeding time includes the grossing time. It is divided in 3 charts:

  • Current: a dial chart displaying the turnaround time of the current month.

  • Annual: a bar chart the historical turnaround time by year.

  • Monthly: a line chart the historical turnaround time by month.

Filter toolbar:

The second toolbar on top of the screen below the quick access toolbar and above the charts, allows you to view the turnaround time by facility, scecialty, subspecialty, procedure or status (or task).
